IMAGE PROCESSING

Different materials reflect different wavelengths of light at different amounts. This idea can be used to scan the surface area of earth to acquire data regarding vegetation, nutrients, moisture and pesticides present on an agricultural land. Once we process the data that the user requests, we model it to onto a map for them to make right decissions on cultivation, buying/ selling of the property.
